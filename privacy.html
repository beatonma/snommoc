Privacy Policy - Commons for Android | snommoc.org

Commons is a personal project by Michael Beaton. Any instance of 'we' in this document refers to me or the Commons app/server/service as a whole - no other humans are involved at this time.

What data is collected?

If you choose to sign in with Google we will retrieve and store a JSON Web Token that can be used to identify your Google account. We retrieve and store only the token - we have no use for your name, email address, or any other profile information.

Once you are signed in, your token will be associated with any content you submit to the Commons service such as comments or votes. Such content may be publicly visible to other users of the Commons app but your token will never be published - instead, a random string of characters will represent your account.

You may delete any content you have submitted via the Commons app. You may also delete your account along with all associated data.

If you do not choose to sign in then you can consider Commons to be read-only: we do not collect or store any data from or about you.

If you send an email your message and address will be accessible only to myself and will be stored only for the purpose of responding to your message. It will not be shared with any other party.

Contact

Please send any queries to contact@snommoc.org.
